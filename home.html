Our Laundry Services

At Spin Bar Laundry, we offer a wide range of laundry services tailored to meet different fabric types, garment needs, and customer preferences. Each service is handled with care, using appropriate washing techniques and detergents to preserve fabric quality and hygiene.

Our wash and fold service is ideal for everyday clothing, ensuring items are thoroughly cleaned, dried, and neatly folded for convenience. For heavier or delicate items such as jackets, sweaters, duvets, and curtains, we apply specialized washing and drying methods that prevent damage and maintain texture.

We also provide express laundry services for customers who require fast turnaround times without compromising on quality. Our ironing services help ensure garments are presentable, wrinkle-free, and ready for use.

To enhance convenience, we offer pickup and delivery services within Limuru town, allowing customers to access professional laundry care without leaving their homes or workplaces.
